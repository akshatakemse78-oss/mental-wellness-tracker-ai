# 🧠 MindMate AI – Exam Wellness Companion

> **Your AI-powered companion for managing stress, building resilience, and staying motivated during competitive exam preparation.**

MindMate AI is a Generative AI-powered wellness tracker designed for students preparing for high-stakes exams like **JEE, NEET, UPSC, CAT, GATE, and CUET**. It transforms daily mood logs and journal entries into personalized wellness insights, helping students identify hidden stress patterns and build healthier study habits.

# 📌 Problem Statement

Students preparing for competitive exams often experience:

* 📚 Academic pressure
* 😔 Self-doubt
* 😰 Stress and anxiety
* 🔥 Burnout
* 😴 Poor sleep quality

MindMate AI acts as an empathetic digital companion that analyzes daily journal entries and mood logs to provide personalized wellness support, mindfulness exercises, and motivational guidance.

## ⭐ Unique Feature – AI Stress Trigger Detector

MindMate AI detects hidden emotional patterns that students often miss.

### Example

**Journal Entry**

> "I studied all day but still feel behind everyone else."

### AI Analysis

**Detected Trigger**

* Fear of Failure
* Comparison with Peers
* Perfectionism

### Suggested Action

* Take a 5-minute breathing break
* Focus on one task at a time
* Celebrate small achievements

# 🛡️ Safety First

MindMate AI is designed as a wellness support tool and **does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment**.

Users experiencing severe emotional distress are encouraged to seek support from trusted individuals or qualified mental health professionals.
